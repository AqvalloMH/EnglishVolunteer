Donation Process

Simple steps to become a blood donor hero

1

Registration

Complete the donor registration form with your personal information and health history.

2

Health Screening

Receive a mini-physical checkup including blood pressure, pulse, and hemoglobin level.

3

Donation

The actual blood donation takes about 8-10 minutes. You'll be seated comfortably during the process.

4

Refreshment

Enjoy snacks and drinks while you rest for 10-15 minutes before resuming your day.
